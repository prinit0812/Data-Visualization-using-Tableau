**Bookstore Business Intelligence Analysis (Tableau)** 

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes bookstore operations data using Tableau to deliver actionable insights for key business stakeholders: Inventory Managers, Finance Managers, and Sales Managers. The goal is to transform raw bookstore data into intuitive visualizations that support data-driven decision-making across inventory planning, financial performance, and sales strategy.

**Data Source**

Bookstore / Library dataset

Includes information on books, authors, genres, publishers, sales, pricing, discounts, inventory levels, and demand patterns

**Stakeholders & Business Focus**

**Inventory Manager**

Analyzed stock levels, demand trends, and inventory turnover

Identified slow-moving and high-demand books

Supported decisions on restocking, inventory optimization, and reducing excess stock

**Finance Manager**
Evaluated revenue, sales contribution, pricing, and discount effectiveness

Analyzed profitability trends across genres, publishers, and authors

Enabled better budgeting, pricing strategy, and financial performance monitoring

**Sales Manager**

Examined sales trends by genre, author, and time

Identified top-performing and underperforming categories

Supported sales strategy, promotions, and demand forecasting

**Key Features**

Multiple interactive Tableau dashboards tailored to each stakeholder

Use of bar charts, line charts, pie charts, KPI indicators, and trend analysis

Filters and parameters for dynamic exploration of data

Clear storytelling through dashboards and storyboards

**Tools & Technologies**

Tableau

Data Visualization & Business Analytics

Stakeholder-driven dashboard design

**Outcome**

This project demonstrates the ability to:

Translate business questions into analytical visualizations

Design stakeholder-specific dashboards

Communicate insights clearly to support operational, financial, and sales decisions
